# Benefits FAQ

## I missed the 31-day window after my qualifying event. Now what?

Elections wait for the next open enrollment (1-15 November), with narrow exceptions for
documented extraordinary circumstances — ask Benefits & Total Rewards.

## How do I know if my event qualifies?

Birth/adoption, marriage/partnership, loss of other coverage, a dependent aging out, and a
move out of your plan's network area all qualify (POL-BEN-041). Ask in Teams and the assistant
checks your specific case.

## Where does the company HSA contribution go?

Into your HSA in January (or prorated from your start date) when you're enrolled in the HDHP —
$750 individual, $1,500 family.

## When does the 401(k) match vest?

25% per year of service, fully vested after 4 years. Your own contributions are always yours.

## Can I cover my parents?

Medical plans cover spouses/partners and children to age 26; parents are not eligible
dependents, but the EAP and backup-care benefits extend to household members.

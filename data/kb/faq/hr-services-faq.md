# HR Services FAQ

## Do I need to open a ticket?

Usually not. Policy answers, request status, letters, and record corrections are all handled
directly in Teams; a ticket exists only when a specialist team takes something on — and then
it's created for you, context attached.

## How fast are letters?

Self-serve letters (employment verification, leave confirmation) generate in under a minute
once you confirm. Custom letters via HR shared services take up to 3 business days.

## Where do I see everything I've asked for?

Ask "what requests do I have open?" — you'll get every case you're allowed to see with stage,
owner, and expected date.

## Is the assistant's answer official?

Answers are grounded on the governed policy library and cite their source. If the assistant
cannot ground an answer, it says so and brings in a person — it does not guess.

## What about my privacy?

The assistant reads only your own record, scoped to you. Every step it takes is written to an
audit ledger your HR team can review.

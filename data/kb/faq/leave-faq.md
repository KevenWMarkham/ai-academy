# Leave & Absence FAQ

## Can I combine leave types?

Yes, in sequence: sick balance first where illness is involved, then PTO, then unpaid or
protected leave per the relevant policy. The Leave Guide computes the best stack for you.

## Do public holidays count against my leave?

No. Company holidays that fall inside a leave period do not draw from the balance.

## What happens to my benefits while on leave?

Benefits continue on paid leave. On unpaid protected leave, coverage continues but your
contribution is collected on return or by arrangement.

## Is my job protected?

Job-protected leave types (parental, caregiver, medical, jury) guarantee return to the same or
an equivalent role. Protection details are confirmed in writing when the leave is approved.

## What is the carryforward rule at year end?

See POL-PTO-014: the standard carryforward cap is 5 days with a 31 March use-by date, and
jurisdiction riders (like California's) can override it.

# How to Check the Status of a Request

## The short version

Ask Copilot in Teams "where is my request?" — it reads every case you're allowed to see and
answers with the stage, the owner, and the expected completion date. Pure read, instant.

## What you can track

Time-off requests, tuition cases, letter requests, data corrections, benefits changes,
accommodations cases — anything with a case number (HRC-…).

## Stages you'll see

Submitted → In review → Manager approval → Specialist queue → Completed. If a case sits in one
stage past its service level, the owner is pinged automatically and you'll see a note.

## If something looks stuck

Nothing to do — stuck cases surface to the owning team automatically. If it's urgent, say so
and the assistant routes an escalation with your case attached.

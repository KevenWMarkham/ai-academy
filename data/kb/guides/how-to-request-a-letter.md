# How to Request an Employment Letter

## Letters you can self-serve

- **Employment verification** — for mortgages, landlords, or visas: confirms role, start date,
  status, and salary band.
- **Leave confirmation** — confirms an approved leave and protected return.

Ask Copilot in Teams — the letter is drafted from your worker record, jurisdiction-checked,
and shown to you for review. You acknowledge; it generates as a PDF and is logged to the
audit trail. Typical time: under a minute.

## What you'll be asked

Only what the template needs. If your record is missing a field, the assistant tells you and
starts a data-correction first — bad data never ships in an official letter.

## Letters that need HR Ops

Custom-content letters, letters naming compensation figures beyond the salary band, and
letters for legal proceedings go to HR shared services with a 3-day service level.

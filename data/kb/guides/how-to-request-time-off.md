# How to Request Time Off

## Steps

1. Open Copilot in Teams (or the HR portal) and say what dates you want.
2. The assistant checks your balance and the team calendar and files the request.
3. Your manager gets an approval card; balances in good standing auto-approve.
4. You get a confirmation with the case number — track it any time by asking
   "where is my request?".

## Lead times

Ask at least 10 business days ahead for 3+ consecutive days (see POL-PTO-014). Single days
need no lead time but courtesy-check your team's calendar.

## Common questions

- **Half days** are supported — say "morning" or "afternoon".
- **Cancelling**: ask to cancel the case number; the balance returns immediately.
- **Sick, not vacation?** Illness uses sick leave, not PTO — just report the absence.

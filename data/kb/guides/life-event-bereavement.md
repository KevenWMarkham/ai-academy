# Life Event Guide: Losing a Loved One

We're sorry. Take the time first — the paperwork can wait, and most of it can be done for you.

## Right away (no forms needed)

Up to **10 paid days** for immediate family, 3 for extended family (POL-LVE-033) — just tell
your manager or Copilot; no documentation is required in the first 10 days. Extra time can be
arranged as PTO or unpaid leave for travel and estate matters.

## In the following weeks

1. **EAP grief counselling** — 6 confidential sessions, free, for you and your household.
2. **Dependents & beneficiaries** — if the loss affects your benefits, it is a qualifying
   event with a 31-day window; the Navigator will walk you through only what applies.
3. **If your loved one was your emergency contact** — a one-line data correction updates it.

## Who to ask

Say "bereavement" in Teams and the Navigator sequences only what's relevant; anything sensitive
routes straight to a human in HR shared services.

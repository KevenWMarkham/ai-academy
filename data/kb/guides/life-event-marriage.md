# Life Event Guide: Marriage & Partnership

Marriage or a registered domestic partnership is a qualifying life event — here is the
sequence, by deadline.

## The deadline-ordered checklist

1. **Within 31 days** — add your spouse/partner to benefits (special enrollment, POL-BEN-041).
2. **Within 45 days** — review beneficiaries and tax withholding; married filing status can
   change your take-home pay.
3. **Within 60 days** — provide the certificate for dependent verification.
4. **If changing your name** — file a data-correction with the certificate; HR Data Services
   updates payroll, benefits, and your directory entry together (5 business days).

## Good to know

Both partners employed here? Each keeps individual benefits or one becomes a dependent —
Copilot can compare the premium impact. A name change never blocks payroll: pay continues
under the verified account while records update.

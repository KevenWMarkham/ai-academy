# Life Event Guide: Moving Home

A move looks small but touches payroll tax, benefits networks, and compliance. In order:

## The deadline-ordered checklist

1. **Within 30 days** — update your address in the worker record (a one-line ask in Teams;
   the data team confirms in 3 business days).
2. **Within 30 days** — confirm the new location is a compliant work location if you work
   remotely (POL-WRK-007); crossing state or country lines needs a quick compliance check.
3. **Within 45 days** — re-check state/country tax withholding; a move can change it.
4. **Open enrollment or QLE** — moving out of your medical plan's network area is itself a
   qualifying event to switch plans.

## If the company asked you to move

See the Relocation Assistance Policy (POL-WRK-009) for the allowance, movers, and temporary
housing — approval must come before any spend.

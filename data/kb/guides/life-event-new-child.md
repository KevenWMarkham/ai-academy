# Life Event Guide: A New Child

Congratulations! A birth, adoption, or placement triggers several deadlines — here they are in
order. Copilot can kick each one off for you.

## The deadline-ordered checklist

1. **Within 30 days** — elect your parental leave dates (POL-LVE-031): 16 weeks primary /
   6 weeks secondary caregiver, up to two blocks in 12 months.
2. **Within 31 days** — add your child to medical/dental (special enrollment, POL-BEN-041).
   Missing this window means waiting for open enrollment.
3. **Within 45 days** — update tax withholding and, if you wish, beneficiaries.
4. **Within 60 days** — provide the birth or adoption certificate for dependent verification.
5. **Any time** — review EAP new-parent resources and the backup-childcare benefit.

## Jurisdiction notes

- **US-CA:** company pay tops up CA Paid Family Leave to 100% of base salary.
- **DE:** Elternzeit and Elterngeld apply in addition — HR provides the state filing pack.

# Payslips & the Payroll Calendar

## Paydays

- **US:** biweekly, every other Friday; 26 pay periods per year.
- **DE:** monthly, on the 25th.
- **MX:** semi-monthly (quincena), on the 15th and last business day.

When a payday falls on a bank holiday, payment lands the prior business day.

## Reading your payslip

Each payslip itemizes base pay, PTO and sick accrual, pre-tax benefit contributions, taxes,
and employer contributions (401(k) match, HSA). Payslips are published in the payroll portal
two days before payday.

## Direct deposit

Set up or change bank details via a data-correction request; changes are verified with a
micro-deposit and take effect the next full payroll cycle.

## Questions about amounts

Simple questions (missing accrual, benefit deduction amounts) can be answered in Teams from
your payslip data. Disputed amounts or anything involving a court order go directly to Payroll
Operations — the assistant routes you with your context attached.

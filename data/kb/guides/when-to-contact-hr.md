# When to Contact HR (and Who Owns What)

Most questions resolve in Teams without a ticket. When a specialist is needed, going to the
right team the first time is what keeps things fast.

## Who owns what

- **Payroll Operations** — pay amounts, deductions, court-ordered withholdings, tax forms
  (3-day service level).
- **Benefits & Total Rewards** — plan questions, enrollment problems, HSA/401(k) (5 days).
- **Employee Relations** — conflicts, complaints, investigations (2 days, confidential).
- **HR Data Services** — record corrections: address, name, bank, contacts (3 days).
- **IT Service Desk** — access, laptops, accounts (1 day).
- **HR shared services** — everything else, and the human catch-all.

## How routing works

You never need to pick from the list above: describe the problem and the assistant either
resolves it from the knowledge base or routes it — with your conversation and context
attached, so you never repeat yourself. Anything it can't ground confidently goes to a person,
not to a guess.

## Sensitive topics

Compensation disputes, legal matters, and anything involving another person go straight to a
human specialist. These are deliberately **not** self-service.

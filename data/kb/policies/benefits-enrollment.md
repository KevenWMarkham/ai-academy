# Benefits Enrollment Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-BEN-041 · **Jurisdictions:** Global, US plan specifics · **Last review:** 2025-10

## Open enrollment

Open enrollment runs each year **1-15 November** for coverage effective 1 January. Elections
roll forward automatically if you take no action, except flexible spending accounts, which
require a fresh election every year.

## Special enrollment (qualifying life events)

A qualifying life event opens a **31-day special enrollment window** to change elections
outside open enrollment. Qualifying events include: birth or adoption of a child, marriage or
domestic partnership, loss of other coverage, and a dependent losing eligibility. Missing the
31-day window means waiting for the next open enrollment.

## New hires

New hires have 31 days from their start date to make first elections; coverage is retroactive
to day one once elected.

## Proof of eligibility

Adding a dependent requires documentation (birth or marriage certificate) within 60 days.
Unverified dependents are removed retroactively.

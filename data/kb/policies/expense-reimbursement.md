# Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-FIN-051 · **Jurisdictions:** Global · **Last review:** 2026-02

## What is reimbursable

Reasonable business travel (economy airfare, standard hotels, ground transport), client meals
within per-diem limits, professional memberships with manager approval, and approved
home-office claims under the Remote & Hybrid Work Policy (POL-WRK-007).

## Submitting a claim

Submit in the expense tool within **60 days** of the spend with itemized receipts for anything
above $25. Claims are reimbursed with the next payroll cycle after manager approval.

## Per-diem limits

Meals: $75/day domestic, $95/day international. Hotel caps vary by city tier and are published
in the travel portal. Alcohol is reimbursable only with client entertainment pre-approval.

## Non-reimbursable

Commuting to your assigned office, traffic fines, personal entertainment, lounge memberships,
and anything claimed later than 60 days without a written exception.

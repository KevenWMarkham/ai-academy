# Health Plans Overview

**Policy ID:** POL-BEN-042 · **Jurisdictions:** US plan lineup · **Last review:** 2025-10

## Medical plans

Two options, both covering preventive care at 100% in network:

- **PPO Plan:** $500 individual deductible, broad network, higher payroll contribution.
- **HDHP + HSA:** $1,800 individual deductible, lower payroll contribution, HSA-eligible with a
  **$750 annual company HSA contribution** ($1,500 family).

## Dental and vision

Dental covers two cleanings per year at 100%, orthodontics at 50% to a $2,000 lifetime maximum.
Vision covers an annual exam and $200 toward frames or lenses per year.

## Who is eligible

All employees working 20+ hours per week, their spouses/domestic partners, and children to age
26. Coverage begins on the first day of employment once elected.

## Costs and payslips

Payroll contributions are pre-tax and itemized on each payslip. Plan comparison sheets and
premium tables live in the benefits portal.

# Jury Duty & Civic Leave Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-LVE-035 · **Jurisdictions:** Global, with US specifics · **Last review:** 2025-10

## Jury service

Employees summoned for jury service receive **fully paid leave for the duration of service**.
Provide the summons to your manager and open a civic-leave case; court-paid juror fees may be
kept by the employee. No PTO is drawn.

## Court appearances

Appearing as a subpoenaed witness is paid civic leave. Appearing as a party to a personal
matter uses PTO or unpaid leave.

## Voting time

Where polls are not open outside working hours, employees may take up to **2 paid hours** to
vote in national, state, or local elections; coordinate timing with your manager.

## Other civic duties

Volunteer firefighting, emergency rescue service, and election-official duty are protected and
paid up to 5 days per year with documentation.

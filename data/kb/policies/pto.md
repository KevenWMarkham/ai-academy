# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-PTO-014 · **Jurisdictions:** Global, with US-CA and DE-BY riders · **Last review:** 2026-01

## Accrual

Full-time employees accrue PTO per pay period, scaling with tenure: 15 days/year (0-2 years),
20 days/year (2-5 years), 25 days/year (5+ years). Accrual appears on each payslip and in the
worker record (`pto_balance_days`). Vacation accrual pauses during unpaid leave.

## Carryover

Employees may carry over up to **5 unused PTO days** into the next calendar year. Days beyond
the carryover cap are forfeited on 31 December, except where local law says otherwise (see
jurisdiction riders). Carryover days must be used by 31 March.

## Jurisdiction riders

- **US-CA (California):** Accrued PTO is treated as earned wages. It does **not** expire — the
  carryover cap does not apply; instead accrual pauses at a ceiling of 1.5× the annual accrual.
  Unused PTO is **paid out in full at termination**.
- **DE-BY (Germany/Bavaria):** Statutory minimum of 20 working days (Bundesurlaubsgesetz)
  applies; carryover into Q1 is permitted only for operational or health reasons.

## Requesting time off

Request through the HR portal or Copilot in Teams at least 10 business days ahead for periods
of 3+ consecutive days. Manager approval is automatic for balances in good standing.

## Cash-out

Voluntary cash-out of PTO is not offered during employment, except where required by law.
At termination, payout follows the jurisdiction rider (paid out in full in US-CA).

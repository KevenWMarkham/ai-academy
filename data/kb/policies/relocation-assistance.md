# Relocation Assistance Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-WRK-009 · **Jurisdictions:** Global · **Last review:** 2025-07

## Eligibility

Company-initiated transfers of 50+ miles and approved employee-initiated transfers to critical
locations. Assistance must be approved before any move-related spend.

## What the package includes

A tiered relocation allowance (by band and distance), a household-goods move through the
contracted mover, 30 days of temporary housing, and one house-hunting trip. International
moves add immigration filing support and tax briefing.

## Repayment

Employees who voluntarily leave within 12 months of a company-funded move repay a prorated
share of the package.

## After you move

Update your address in the worker record within 30 days (see the data-correction guide), and
re-check your tax withholding and benefits — a move can be a qualifying life event.

# Retirement & 401(k) Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-BEN-043 · **Jurisdictions:** US · **Last review:** 2025-09

## Company match

The company matches **100% of the first 4%** of eligible pay you contribute, per pay period,
with an annual true-up for uneven contribution schedules.

## Enrollment and auto-escalation

New hires are auto-enrolled at 4% pre-tax after 30 days unless they opt out. Contributions
auto-escalate 1% each April to a 10% ceiling; you can change your rate any time.

## Vesting

Employee contributions are always 100% yours. Company match vests 25% per year of service,
fully vested after 4 years.

## Roth and catch-up

A Roth 401(k) option is available. Employees aged 50+ may make catch-up contributions to the
IRS limit. Investment lineup changes and fund fact sheets are published in the retirement
portal each quarter.

# Sick Leave Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-LVE-034 · **Jurisdictions:** Global, with US-CA and DE riders · **Last review:** 2026-01

## Entitlement

Full-time employees receive **10 paid sick days per calendar year** (`sick_balance_days` in the
worker record), available from day one of employment. Sick days do not accrue and reset each
1 January; there is no year-end carryforward and no payout at termination.

## When to use sick leave

Personal illness, injury, medical and dental appointments, and preventive care. Sick leave may
also cover caring for an ill dependent for up to 3 of the 10 days — longer caregiving needs
move to the Caregiver & Medical Leave Policy (POL-LVE-032).

## Reporting an absence

Tell your manager before your normal start time and log the absence in the HR portal or
Copilot in Teams. A medical certificate is required from the **4th consecutive** working day.

## Jurisdiction riders

- **US-CA:** Local ordinances may grant additional protected sick time; the system applies the
  most generous rule automatically.
- **DE:** Statutory sick pay (Entgeltfortzahlung, 6 weeks at 100%) applies; from week 7 the
  health insurer's Krankengeld takes effect. A doctor's note (AU) is required from day 4.

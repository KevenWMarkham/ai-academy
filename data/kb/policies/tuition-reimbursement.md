# Tuition Reimbursement Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-LRN-022 · **Jurisdictions:** Global · **Last review:** 2025-09

## Benefit

Up to **$5,250 per calendar year** for approved, job-related degree programs, certifications,
and courses at accredited institutions. Passing grade (C / equivalent or better) required.

## Process

1. Pre-approval by your manager **before** enrollment (opens a tuition case).
2. Complete the course; submit the grade report and itemized receipt within 60 days.
3. Benefits review verifies eligibility; payroll reimburses in the next cycle.

## Conditions

Employees who voluntarily leave within 12 months of a reimbursement repay 50%. The benefit
requires 6 months of tenure and good performance standing.

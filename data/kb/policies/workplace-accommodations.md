# Workplace Accommodations Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-WRK-008 · **Jurisdictions:** Global, ADA specifics in the US · **Last review:** 2025-08

## Our commitment

The company provides reasonable accommodations so every employee can perform the essential
functions of their role — covering disability, pregnancy, religious observance, and recovery
from illness or injury.

## Requesting an accommodation

Open an accommodations case in the HR portal or ask your HRBP; no diagnosis is shared with your
manager — only the accommodation itself. HR responds within **5 business days** with next steps
and an interim arrangement where possible.

## Examples

Ergonomic equipment, screen readers and assistive software, modified schedules, reserved
parking, a private space for medical needs or prayer, and temporary duty adjustments during
recovery.

## Interactive process

Accommodations are a dialogue: HR, the employee, and (where needed) occupational health iterate
until an effective arrangement is found, documented, and reviewed every 12 months.

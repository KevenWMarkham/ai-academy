# Bereavement Leave Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-LVE-033 · **Jurisdictions:** Global · **Last review:** 2025-11

## Entitlement

- **Immediate family** (spouse/partner, child, parent, sibling): up to **10 paid days**.
- **Extended family** (grandparent, grandchild, in-law): up to **3 paid days**.
- Additional unpaid time or PTO may be arranged with your manager for travel or estate matters.

## Support

No documentation is required in the first 10 days. The Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
offers grief counselling (6 sessions, confidential, free). Life-event support can also help
with beneficiary updates and dependent changes that may follow a loss.

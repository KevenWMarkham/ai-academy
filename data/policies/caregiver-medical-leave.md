# Caregiver & Medical Leave Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-LVE-032 · **Jurisdictions:** Global, with US-CA rider · **Last review:** 2025-11

## Family caregiver leave

Employees may take up to **12 weeks of job-protected leave** in a rolling 12-month period to
care for a spouse, child, or parent with a serious health condition (FMLA-equivalent, applied
globally as company policy). The first 2 weeks are paid at 100%; the remainder is unpaid unless
a jurisdiction rider provides pay.

## Employee medical leave

Personal medical leave follows the same 12-week protection. Short-term disability covers 60%
of base salary from week 3 where enrolled.

## US-CA rider

California Paid Family Leave (PFL) provides up to **8 weeks of partial wage replacement** for
caregiving, which runs concurrently with company caregiver leave. CFRA protections apply.

## How to start a leave

1. Tell your manager and open a leave case (Copilot in Teams or the HR portal).
2. Provide certification of the family member's condition within 15 days.
3. HR confirms eligibility, pay treatment and protected dates in writing.
4. Sick balance (`sick_balance_days`) and then PTO may be applied to unpaid periods.

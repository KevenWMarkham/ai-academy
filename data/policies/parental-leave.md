# Parental Leave Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-LVE-031 · **Jurisdictions:** Global, with US-CA and DE riders · **Last review:** 2025-11

## Entitlement

- **Primary caregiver:** 16 weeks paid leave at 100% of base salary.
- **Secondary caregiver:** 6 weeks paid leave at 100% of base salary.
- Leave may be taken in up to two blocks within 12 months of the birth, adoption, or placement.

## Election window

Employees must elect parental leave dates within **30 days** of the qualifying event (birth,
adoption or placement). New dependents must be added to benefits within the **31-day special
enrollment window** — missing it means waiting for open enrollment.

## Jurisdiction riders

- **US-CA (California):** Company leave runs concurrently with CA Paid Family Leave (PFL) and
  FMLA/CFRA where eligible; the company tops up state PFL benefit to 100% of base salary.
- **DE (Germany):** Elternzeit (parental leave) rights under BEEG apply in addition; up to
  3 years unpaid job-protected leave per child, with Elterngeld administered by the state.

## Checklist on a new child

1. Notify HR of the qualifying event (starts the 30-day election clock).
2. Add the dependent to medical/dental within 31 days.
3. Elect leave dates with your manager and HR.
4. Update tax withholding and beneficiaries if desired.
5. Review the Employee Assistance Program (EAP) new-parent resources.

# Remote & Hybrid Work Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-WRK-007 · **Jurisdictions:** Global · **Last review:** 2026-02

## Hybrid baseline

Roles designated hybrid require 2 anchor days per week in the assigned office. Fully remote
arrangements need VP approval and a compliant work location on file.

## Equipment stipend

Remote and hybrid employees receive a one-time **$500 home-office equipment stipend** (or local
equivalent), claimable through expenses within 90 days of start or designation. A replacement
stipend is available every 36 months.

## Work location changes

Working more than 20 business days per year from a location other than your registered one
(including abroad) requires prior approval — tax and immigration exposure is assessed first.
Update your address in the worker record within 30 days of moving.
